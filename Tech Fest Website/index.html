About the Fest

ABC University Tech Fest is a celebration of creativity, curiosity, and collaboration. We invite you to join a vibrant community of students and innovators for thrilling coding contests, ingenious robotics battles, inspiring guest lectures, and lively cultural events. At Tech Fest, knowledge meets excitement—everyone’s welcome to explore, compete, and have fun!
